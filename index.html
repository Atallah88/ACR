About

Welcome to Al Rayyan Football Academy, located in Kuwait. We are dedicated to providing professional football training for players aged 17 and above. Our academy focuses on developing players’ skills through training sessions, matches, and tournaments. With experienced coaches and regular training on the field in Om Al-Haiman, we strive to push our players to their full potential. We also organize seasonal training camps in Qatar, offering players a unique opportunity to enhance their abilities and experience top-level football environments.
